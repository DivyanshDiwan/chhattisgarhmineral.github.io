Coal Resources Spread Across Chhattisgarh

The State has 18% of total deposits of Coal in India. 54912 million tonnes Coal has been estimated in 13 Coal field of the State occurring in Raigarh, Surguja, Korea, Balrampur, Surajpur and Korba District.

Most of the Coal deposits are of power grade. NTPC and CSEB in Korba are the major producer of thermal power in the State and a new plant NTPC is completed in Seepat, Bilaspur.
